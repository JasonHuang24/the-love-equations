Profile A - Self Assessment
Map your market position across five factors.
This calculator scores Looks, Money, Status, Charm, and Exposure through thirty prompts (twenty-eight scored) weighted by how directly each one tends to move attention, leverage, and realistic options. Wherever a factor can be counted, you enter the real number and it is scored on a percentile-anchored curve — self-report is reserved for the few things only you can observe. Answer by current reality, not potential.
