Deep Dives
Longform essays that take one question and follow it all the way down. Where the cards state a claim in a sentence, a Deep Dive gives it the room to breathe — the history, the evidence, and the framework, laid out in full.

Each essay stands on its own and extends the shorter cards elsewhere on the site. Sourced history and statistics are marked as Records; the site’s own speculative reads are marked as Frameworks — so you always know which is which.
