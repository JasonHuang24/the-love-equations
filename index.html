What this is

Desire, described the way it behaves.

The Love Equations maps attraction as it actually works — not as we wish it did, and not as the angriest corners of the internet insist. It treats desire as a market with real thresholds, grades every claim by how good the evidence is, and keeps the human center the math leaves out.

Market, not morality

Every score here reads leverage, attention, and options — never a person’s worth.

Evidence, graded

Claims are tagged Solid, Mixed, Contested, or Lens — sourced where it can be, hedged where it can’t.

Neither rage nor cope

See the market clearly, build where building is real, and refuse both bitterness and delusion.
